Manufacture 4.0

Driving the Future of Smart Manufacturing

With more than 20 years of expertise in aerospace parts manufacturing, Pudak Aerospace is embracing the future through Manufacture 4.0. Starting in mid-2024, we began integrating digitalization and automation across every division, step by step, to transform our operations. This shift toward smart manufacturing enables us to improve efficiency, precision, and innovation at every stage of production. Our goal is to fully modernize our processes, ensuring we stay ahead in the aerospace industry while delivering superior quality and performance for our customers.

Our journey toward digital transformation is powered by two key innovations:

Odoo ERP Integration

We have implemented Odoo as our central digital platform to streamline operations, improve efficiency, and ensure real-time data visibility across all departments.

Sentry: Our Digital Transformation Platform

To further support modern manufacturing, we developed Sentry, a proprietary platform designed to enhance connectivity, improve workflow automation, and drive continuous improvement across our processes.

By combining Odoo ERP and Sentry, we are shaping a smarter, more efficient, and environmentally responsible future for our manufacturing operations.

Precision Our Passion

Passion

At Pudak Aerospace, precision is more than just a goal—it's our passion. Every step of our manufacturing process, from concept and design to production and final inspection, is driven by an uncompromising commitment to accuracy and reliability, ensuring every aerospace part we produce exceeds industry standards.

Embracing the era of Industry 4.0, we integrate cutting-edge digital technologies such as advanced CAD/CAM systems, real-time data monitoring, and automated quality control to enhance every aspect of production. These innovations allow us to achieve unmatched consistency, reduce lead times, and maintain full traceability across the manufacturing cycle.

By combining our engineering expertise with digitalization, we not only improve efficiency but also ensure that each component meets the rigorous demands of modern aerospace engineering. This forward-thinking approach enables us to deliver solutions that not only meet today's requirements but also anticipate the needs of tomorrow's aerospace industry.

Above all, our mission goes beyond manufacturing. We are dedicated to building long-term partnerships with our clients, understanding their unique challenges, and delivering precision-driven solutions that empower them to reach new heights.

CNC: 5 Axis Machining

High Precision

    At Pudak Aerospace, we utilize advanced 5-axis machining technology to deliver precision parts faster and more efficiently. Key advantages include:


  1. Faster Production - Complex components and prototypes can be produced in just 1-2 weeks instead of months.
  2. Higher Accuracy - Fewer setups mean less risk of alignment errors and greater machining precision.
  3. Better Surface Finish - Shorter cutting tools reduce vibration, improving quality and minimizing hand-finishing work.
  4. Design Flexibility - Complex geometries can be machined directly from solid material without the need for initial castings.

    5. With 5-axis machining, we combine speed, precision, and innovation to meet the demanding needs of aerospace manufacturing.

EDM (Electrical Discharge Machining)

Complex Geometry

    At Pudak Aerospace, we use advanced Electrical Discharge Machining (EDM) technology to produce high-precision components with complex shapes that traditional machining cannot achieve. EDM uses controlled electrical discharges to erode the material, creating intricate details with exceptional accuracy.


    EDM Capabilities

  • Wire EDM - Perfect for precision tools and dies, using a thin wire electrode to cut complex shapes.
  • Ideal for plastic molds and deep cavities, using custom-shaped electrodes to form intricate details.

    • HQSF Technology by Makino

    Our High Quality Surface Finish (HQSF) technology enhances EDM performance by using a special dielectric fluid additive, producing surfaces far smoother than conventional EDM methods.

MPI (Magnetic Particle Inspection)

Quality Control

    At Pudak Aerospace, ensuring the integrity and reliability of every component is paramount. Magnetic Particle Inspection (MPI) is a critical nondestructive testing (NDT) method we employ to detect surface and near-surface discontinuities in ferromagnetic materials such as iron, nickel, cobalt, and their alloy.


  • Direct Magnetization - An electric current is passed directly through the component, generating a magnetic field within the material. The magnetic lines of force are oriented perpendicular to the current flow and may utilize alternating current (AC) or direct current (DC).
  • Indirect Magnetization - A magnetic field is applied externally without passing current through the component, magnetizing it indirectly.
